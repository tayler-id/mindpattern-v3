# Editorial Taxonomy

Derived from the historical editorial research.

## Framework families and selection

| When you need | Framework | Evidence grammar |
|---|---|---|
| Low-heat civic correction | civic-manners editorial | anecdote, observation, social norm |
| Satirical exposure of official logic | satirical pseudo-policy indictment | parody of official logic, pseudo-quantification, controlled inversion |
| Principled refutation | rights-first philosophical rebuttal | definitions, contradiction, precedent, institutional critique |
| Accountability under evidentiary risk | open-letter charge sheet | named actors, records, procedures, documented actions |
| Rapid mass persuasion | mobilization pamphlet | moral claims plus practical logistics |
| Recurring attention engine | serial agitation column | episodic narrative, cumulative evidence, audience callbacks |
| Moral legitimacy and refusal logic | conscience-first argument | lived experience, moral law, strategic reasoning |
| Record-based overthrow of a dominant myth | evidence-led myth-busting indictment | aggregated cases, timelines, traceable facts |
